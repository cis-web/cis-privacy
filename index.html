Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CIS Sulaimany ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

The CIS Sulaimany app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app is designed exclusively for internal use by staff and teachers of the Computer Institute of Sulaimany.

2. Use of Data

All data displayed within the app is managed internally by the institute's administration. No user data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3. Authentication

The app uses Firebase Authentication for secure login. Login credentials are managed solely by the institute administration and are not shared with any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase – for database and authentication services. Firebase's privacy policy is available at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Cookies

The app does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. The app uses industry-standard security measures to protect data within the institute's management system.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
